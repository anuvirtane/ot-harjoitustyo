Preparations (d0) :


If possible, soak the lentils over the previous night or on the same morning. 
* Chop the carrots and onions.


Cooking (immediately after afternoon sit) :


1. Sauté the onions over medium heat
2. Add carrots and spices and keep frying 5min or so
3. Add lentils and mix, then add broth - and water if needed - to cover the mix 
4. Bring to a boil over med-high heat while mixing a few times. Reduce heat and let simmer until the lentils are tender.
5. Check taste and correct with salt, pepper and spices if necessary. 


Note : If there are leftovers, you may freeze it until the end of the course
